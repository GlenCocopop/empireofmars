Proclamation from the Throne

Welcome to the official website of the Empire of Mars, established under the sovereign rule of Glen Coco I, Imperator Marsialis. Here, we celebrate the unity, strength, and unparalleled ambition of the Martian people, who stand as the vanguard of humanity's future.

Through the Emperor’s wisdom and unwavering leadership, the Red Planet has risen to unmatched glory. Mars is not merely a colony; it is a beacon of hope, a testament to human greatness, and the foundation of an empire destined to span the stars.

We invite you to explore the Principalities, study the Constitution, and take your place among the proud citizens of Mars. Together, we shall forge a legacy that will echo across the cosmos.
