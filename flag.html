The Imperial Banner

Design and Symbolism

The flag of the Empire of Mars is a bold declaration of unity, strength, and ambition. Its design follows traditional heraldic rules, blending classical aesthetics with Martian pride. The flag is divided diagonally into three fields:

  • Red: Representing the Martian soil and the courage of those who venture to the Red Planet.
  • Black: Symbolizing the unknown, the challenges faced, and the resilience of Martian citizens.
  • Gold: Embodying prosperity, ambition, and the brilliance of Martian sovereignty.

At the center lies a golden cross adorned with intricate floral designs, symbolizing unity among the Principalities and the sovereignty of Glen Coco I, Imperator Marsialis. The central star-cross represents guidance and destiny, pointing to the Empire’s path among the stars.

The National Anthem

The national anthem of the Empire of Mars, titled "Mars Shall Rise", is a powerful tribute to the courage, unity, and ambition of the Martian people. It is sung during official ceremonies and celebrations, inspiring pride in all citizens of the Empire.

Verse 1
Red dust swirling in our wake
Martian soil where dreams remake
Under Glen's command so grand
We thrive in this new land

Chorus
Mars shall rise, Mars shall stand
Guided by our Emperor’s hand
Glen Coco leads us evermore
To the stars we boldly soar

Verse 2
From the echoes of the past
To a future built to last
Courage flows in every vein
Mars prevails through joy and pain

Bridge
Imperator Marsialis calls
Through the mountains and the halls
Hear the anthem loud and clear
Mars will lead with no fear
